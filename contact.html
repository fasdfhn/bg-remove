Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create a white wallpaper with BG Swap?

Simply upload your image to BG Swap, and our AI will remove the background. You can then download your subject on a transparent background and place it on any white wallpaper design.

Can I make checkered wallpaper backgrounds with BG Swap?

Yes! After removing your image background, you can place your subject on any checkered wallpaper pattern for creative designs and visual impact.

What file formats are supported for white wallpaper creation?

BG Swap supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP image formats, perfect for creating high-quality white wallpapers and checkered wallpapers.

Do you offer bulk processing for wallpaper designers?

Yes, you can upload multiple images at once for background removal, making it easy to create collections of white wallpapers and checkered wallpapers.
